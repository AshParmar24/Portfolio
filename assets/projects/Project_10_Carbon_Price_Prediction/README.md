# Carbon Price Forecasting

Forecasting carbon market prices using market drivers, XGBoost and SHAP explainability.

## Overview

**Domain:** Energy Markets / Forecasting

**Business Value:** Supports carbon market risk analysis, hedging decisions and sustainability strategy.

## Key Skills

- XGBoost
- Time-Series Validation
- Carbon Markets
- SHAP
- Forecasting

## Project Highlights

- Modelled carbon prices using energy and market drivers
- Used time-series-aware validation to reduce look-ahead bias
- Applied SHAP to explain forecast drivers
- Connected predictions to market and risk management use cases
